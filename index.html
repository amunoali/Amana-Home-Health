Services that keep your loved ones safe, at home.

We believe that being surrounded by the people and places you care about is the most effective way to recuperate. Our HHAs and RNs collaborate to provide outstanding in-home care, ensuring that we can:

  • Maintain a safe and comfortable environment for seniors at home.
  • Provide medically fragile people with round-the-clock clinical care.
  • Enable families & guardians to return to work and routine

    Our Services

    Amana Home Health CareServices

    Our organization assists in making life at home easier and more enjoyable. We work hard to meet our clients' home health care needs and to help them maintain their independence. You may rely on us to provide the necessary care for your loved ones.

    Personal Care & Living Assistance

    Companionship, comfort, and assistance in the home.Amana Home Care provides elders with dignified personal care, cheerful companionship, and light housekeeping assistance. We ensure that seniors can remain at home in comfort and safety, knowing that they will get regular planned support.

    Specialized Therapies

    Amana therapists use a variety of therapies to help you recover and improve at your own speed. Our therapists are completely certified and trained to assist you or a loved one in receiving the full advantages of therapy without having to leave the comfort of your own home.

    Skilled Nursing Services

    Clinical care with compassion for a faster recovery. Our caring and well-trained RNs and LPNs are certified to provide a broad variety of clinical and medical services to elders. Amana RNs assist at-home comfort and recovery with better quality standards by coordinating with your medical team and existing plan of care.

    In-Home Care

    Comfort Keepers creates personalized care programs that address physical safety, emotional well-being, and sociability. Our mission is to assist our clients in achieving the highest possible quality of life while remaining independent at home. Our senior home care plans are tailored to the specific needs of each family and household.

    Respite Care

    Our senior clients' lives, as well as the lives of their family, are improved by Respite Care. Our expert Caregivers work relentlessly to make a difference in the lives of the older persons in our care, whether it is for 4 hours a day or 24 hours a day.

    Live-In or 24-Hour Care

    Live-in care allows seniors to age in situ, in the comfort of their own homes, surrounded by their treasured memories. A excellent alternative to assisted living facilities or retirement homes is round-the-clock care.
