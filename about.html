Who We Are

Fostering Independence,
Improving Lives.

mana Home Care is home care founded by with the intent that every life has value. We strive to provide a safe, welcoming, comfortable place to call home and essential to living a life of independence.
Amana Home Care is driven in providing our patients the personal care they need in order for them to continue to live dignified and independent lives in the comfort of their own homes.
We begin by assessing and understand our patient's individual needs and met those needs through selectively assigning qualifies, trustworthy and compassionate care aides.

  1. Accommodate individual residents' changing needs and preferences
  2. Maximize residents' choice, dignity, autonomy, privacy independence, quality of life, and safety
  3. While structured differently to best meet individual needs as well as to comply with state regulations, all of our aides offer high-quality services with the shared goal of supporting individuals to stive in the community.

Our personal care attendants provide multiple services for our clients, ranging from meal preparation, house cleaning, activities of daily living (ADLs) such as bathing, grooming, and dressing. Amana Home Care also provides medication set-ups and administration under the supervision of a registered nurse.
