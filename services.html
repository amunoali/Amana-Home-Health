Care Giving

Our agency have strict procedures for screening caregivers. Families can rest assured that when a caregiver shows up at your door, she/he has been thoroughly scrutinized.

Care Team

The Endorsements

Skilled Nursing Services

Clinical care with compassion for a faster recovery. Our caring and well-trained RNs and LPNs are certified to provide a broad variety of clinical and medical services to elders. Amana RNs assist at-home comfort and recovery with better quality standards by coordinating with your medical team and existing plan of care.

Home Health Aide Services

Comfort Keepers creates personalized care programs that address physical safety, emotional well-being, and sociability. Our mission is to assist our clients in achieving the highest possible quality of life while remaining independent at home. Our senior home care plans are tailored to the specific needs of each family and household.

Specialized Therapies

Amana therapists use a variety of therapies to help you recover and improve at your own speed. Our therapists are completely certified and trained to assist you or a loved one in receiving the full advantages of therapy without having to leave the comfort of your own home.
